Rafting Site Plan

Jose Cardona

Division

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to market our rafting venue to people all over the world. It will provide detailed information about our rafting trips, including the different packages we offer, safety measures, and customer testimonials. The website will also offer an easy way for potential customers to book their adventures online.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts of all ages who are interested in experiencing rafting. This includes families looking for fun activities, thrill-seekers looking for exciting experiences, and individuals or groups interested in team-building exercises. We aim to attract both seasoned rafters and beginners by highlighting the safety and enjoyment of our trips.

Branding

Website Logo

The logo for our website features a dynamic and bold design that reflects the adventurous spirit of rafting. It uses vibrant colors to convey excitement and energy, and the imagery includes elements such as rapids and paddles to emphasize the rafting theme.

Style Guide

Color Palette

Our color palette is designed to evoke the natural beauty and thrill of rafting. The primary colors include deep blues and greens to represent water and nature, with accent colors like bright oranges and yellows to capture energy and excitement. You can view our complete color palette here: Coolors Color Palette.

Typography

Headings Example Font

We use a bold, sans-serif font for headings to ensure they stand out and are easily readable. This font adds a modern and clean look to our website, making important information immediately noticeable.
